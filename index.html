üß© The Problem

Late-life immigrants are underserved

Many seniors immigrate later and face language barriers, fewer social supports, and difficulty navigating primary care. Prevention becomes reactive ‚Äî people seek help only when symptoms worsen.

Social determinants drive outcomes

Income, housing, food access, transportation, isolation, and health literacy often matter as much as medical history ‚Äî but most tools ignore them.
